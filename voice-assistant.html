My simpleVoice Assistant

Android app prototype

Lyra is the prototype of voice assistant that can help you with some routines. It can greet you, tell you the time and weather in your region and make an appointment reminder.

It is only basic project that was used for educational purposes while trying to figure out mobile development patterns.

The app is written on pure Java using such instruments as Android Studio, Figma & Adobe Illustrator.
