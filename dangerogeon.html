The age of Dangerogeon

Rouge-like strategy game

Dangerogeon is the first game that I've created with my team called "Hellas". Initially it was just an idea of our project for Samsung IT Academy, but later on it has grown to something more serious and valuable.

We were eager to create a mobile game for anyone who has a nostalgia for the genre of rouge-like games.

Although it seemed hard at the beginning, we have created our own new world - the world of monsters, dangers, dungeons and, of course, heroes. The main idea is to defeat bad guys, open chests, earn trophies, buy better equipment - and going on the adventures again!

The game is written on pure Java using such instruments as Android studio, FL Studio, Adobe Illustrator, Krita and other.
